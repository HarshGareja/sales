Welcome to Pretty Rugged: Where Style Meets Durability

At Pretty Rugged, we believe that fashion and function should go hand in hand. Our mission is to create products that not only look great but also stand up to the demands of everyday life. As featured on Shark Tank, our collection is designed to offer the perfect blend of style, durability, and comfort, making it the ideal choice for those who refuse to compromise.

Our Story: From Concept to Shark Tank

Pretty Rugged was born out of a desire to create products that cater to the adventurous spirit while maintaining a sense of style. Our founder, inspired by a love for the outdoors and a passion for fashion, set out to design a line of products that could withstand the elements without sacrificing aesthetics. This vision caught the attention of Shark Tank, where our products were showcased to a national audience, earning praise for their innovative design and quality.

Rugged Blankets: Perfect for Outdoor Adventures

Our rugged blankets are a cornerstone of the Pretty Rugged collection. Crafted with high-quality materials, these blankets are designed to provide warmth and resilience in any setting. Whether you're camping in the mountains or enjoying a picnic in the park, our rugged blankets are the perfect companion.

Each blanket is available in a variety of colors and patterns, allowing you to choose the perfect match for your style. The durable construction ensures that your blanket will last for years, providing comfort and warmth on all your adventures.

Waterproof Fur Jackets: Stay Dry and Cozy

Our waterproof fur jackets are another standout product, offering protection and style in any weather. These jackets are designed with cutting-edge waterproof technology, ensuring that you stay dry even in the heaviest rain. The luxurious fur lining provides added warmth, making these jackets a must-have for the colder months.

The design of our waterproof fur jackets is both practical and fashionable, allowing you to transition seamlessly from outdoor activities to urban settings. Whether you're hiking in the wilderness or strolling through the city, our jackets keep you looking stylish and feeling comfortable.

Pretty Rugged Capes: Versatile and Stylish

Our Pretty Rugged capes are a versatile addition to any wardrobe. These capes are designed to offer both style and functionality, making them perfect for a variety of occasions. Whether you're dressing up for a night out or layering for a casual day, our capes provide the perfect finishing touch.
